About Us

Based in Hyderabad, 10TV is a Telugu News and Current Affairs Channel owned by Spoorthi Communications Private Limited. Launched in March 2013, 10TV primarily caters to the Telugu TV audience in the states both Telangana and Andhra Pradesh, and has good news network in both the states.
10TV has two modern online studios and fully automated AVID Newsroom Computer System (NRCS). The channel has modern support systems and technical infrastructure to cover the news from all the locations in the two Telugu states. The channel uplinking is done through IS17 satellite. 10TV is available across all major cable and DTH platforms in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana.
10TV firmly believes in providing credible, consistent and comprehensive news coverage to its audience. 10TV is backed by the promoters with good reputation and credibility.
